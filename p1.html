Key Features:

  • Open Design with Ventilation Holes
  • Lightweight and Portable
  • Clip-on design for airflow and easy use
  • Easy Access to Buttons, Ports and Pins

The first prototype was a simple, open design with holes for ventilation and lots of access.

Pros:

  • Lightweight and modern, outside of the box design
  • Excellent ventilation
  • Easy to assemble and disassemble
  • Full access to all components

Cons:

  • Less secure than enclosed designs
  • More susceptible to dust and debris
  • Pins fully exposed and at risk of damage
  • Buttons placed incorrectly

Improvement Opportunities:

  • Modify the design for the Pins to sit securely
  • Reposition the buttons for better accessibility
