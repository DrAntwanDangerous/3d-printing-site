Prototype 3

Please click on the image for an enlarged version.

Key Features:

  • Snug fit
  • Ample safety and pin access
  • Clip on redesign
  • Good ventilation

The third prototype took the best measurements from prototype 2 and made further improvements. I knew the fit was good and made a bridge with fully enclosed pins and wide access for female ports. I kept the clip on idea and made a large opening for buttons and ventilation. The design is almost fully enclosed and will protect the main components well.

Pros:

  • Took the best of design 2 and made it better
  • Fits snug and securely and now has wider openings
  • Easy to assemble and disassemble
  • Pins fully secure when not in use and when in use access points are wide and easy to reach
  • Lid resizable for a secure fit.

Cons:

  • Wasn't fully enclosed
  • Required more room for the usb port
  • Lid was too tight
