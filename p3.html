Prototype 3

Please click on the image for an enlarged version.

Key Features:

  • Snug fit
  • Ample safety and pin access
  • Clip-on redesign
  • Good ventilation

The third prototype took the best measurements from prototype 2 and made further improvements. I knew the fit was good and created a bridge design with fully enclosed pins and wide access for ports. I kept the clip-on concept and added a larger opening for buttons and ventilation. The design is almost fully enclosed and protects the main components effectively.

Pros:

  • Improves on Prototype 2
  • Snug fit with wider access points
  • Easy assembly and disassembly
  • Pins are protected but still accessible
  • Adjustable lid for secure fit

Cons:

  • Not fully enclosed
  • Needed more space for USB port
  • Lid was too tight
