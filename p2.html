Prototype 2

Please click on the image for an enlarged version.

Key Features:

  • Open Design with Ventilation Holes
  • Pins and board sit snugly
  • Clip-on design for airflow and easy use
  • Improved accessibility to components

The second prototype was very similar to prototype 1. The client Minh requested some modifications. I remade the unit with design changes, widening the pin access area and remeasuring so the chip sat snug and secure. I added improved USB support to increase functionality. I liked the unique, out-of-the-box design, but the client felt the microchip was too exposed. I did receive positive feedback for creating a design that stood out from others in the class.

Pros:

  • Added support for the USB insert
  • Fits snug and securely
  • Easy to assemble and disassemble
  • Pins fully accessible

Cons:

  • Less secure than enclosed designs
  • Client requested full pin protection
  • Supports were too weak
  • Buttons placed incorrectly

Improvement Opportunities:

  • Enclose the unit to protect pins
  • Reposition the buttons
  • Strengthen USB supports
