Prototype 2

Please click on the image for an enlarged version.

Key Features:

  • Open Design with Ventilation Holes
  • Pins and board sit snugly
  • Clip-on design for airflow and easy use
  • Improved accessibility to components

The second prototype was very similar to prototype 1, the client Minh requested some modifications. I remade the unit with some design changes, widening the pin access area and remeasuring so the chip was snug and secure; I added a different usb support and decided to do this to improve functionality. I liked the out of the box design but the client did not approve of it as the microchip was too heavily exposed. I did recieve praise for my efforts as my design was unlike any other in the class.

Pros:

  • Added support for the usb insert
  • Fits snug and securely
  • Easy to assemble and disassemble
  • Pins fully accessible

Cons:

  • Less secure than enclosed designs
  • Client requested the pins to be fully protected
  • Supports were too weak
  • Buttons placed incorrectly

Improvement Opportunities:

  • Enclosing the unit so its secure and the pins are safe
  • Reposition the buttons again
  • Strengthen the usb supports
