Assessment Task 3

Mission Brief

The ESP32-C3 Zero Microcontroller is a light and portable unit used for a variety of projects. Design teams were asked to create a secure case that allowed ventilation and full access to buttons, ports, and pins.

Three prototypes were created per student.

The first prototype was a simple, open design. The second improved measurements and added a clip lid. The third was a sleek, compact case. Each prototype was tested for durability, functionality, and usability. The final design was chosen based on balance of aesthetics and performance.
