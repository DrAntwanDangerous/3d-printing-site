Final Design

Please click on the image for an enlarged version.

Key Features:

  • Snug fit
  • Ample safety and pin access
  • Fully enclosed unit
  • Good ventilation

The final prototype was selected based on its balance of aesthetics, functionality, and user-friendliness. It provides strong ventilation while maintaining full access to pins and ports. The design is lightweight, durable, and secure.

Pros:

  • Upgraded design based on feedback
  • Fits securely
  • Easy to assemble and disassemble
  • All pins fully functional

The earlier prototypes were close to complete and only required minor adjustments. Improvements were made by refining the fit and slightly modifying certain parts to enhance usability and durability. This final design meets all client requirements and represents a polished result of the team’s collaboration and design process. It demonstrates strong teamwork, effective communication, and iterative improvement. Overall, this is the most refined and successful version of the project.
